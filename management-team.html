Our Management Team

Much like our portfolio companies, we also aim to maximize synergies when it comes to our team. Our executive team consists of a diverse group of experts that all bring different skill sets, academic backgrounds, and experiences to the table. They are highly capable individuals that are even stronger together. Their different perspectives and insights allow for the innovative and solution-focused thinking needed to overcome business challenges and foster growth.

Mr. Mahmood Hussain

Chief Financial Officer

With over 2 decades of local and international experience in M&A and private equity transactions, restructuring, and accounting advisory, Mr. Hussain has a wealth of knowledge —making him highly valued in his role.

Mr. Wilfried Hugebaert

Senior Vice President Logistics

With international experience in the Middle East, Africa and CIS, in Strategy, Growth and Operations management in the express courier, freight forwarding, project logistics, warehousing, trucking, container services and End-to-End logistics; Mr. Hugebaert has a “Can-Do, Make-Do” mindset to strive logistics growth for SISCO holding and will foster all logistics initiatives to reach the 6x26 strategy.

Mr. Christophe Durand

Head of Mergers and Acquisitions

With over 22 years of experience in investments and transactions in Europe, Middle East and Africa, Mr. Durand aims at leading and supporting the future acquisitions of SISCO holding.

Mr. Richard Fourie

Chief Corporate Affairs Officer

With international experience in management consultancy, civil engineering, and manufacturing, Mr. Fourie has the know-how and strategic, entrepreneurial mindset to drive impactful growth for SISCO holding.

Dr. Saleem Sheikh

Chief Legal Counsel

With years of experience in corporate law, numerous published works including eight law books, and multiple international lectures in commercial law under his belt, Dr. Saleem is a true expert in all commercial matters of the law.

Mr. Feras Halawani

FChief Audit Executive

Equipped with a wealth of Internal Audit global experience, Mr. Feras provides objective assurance and advisory insight on risk management, internal control, and governance processes, to SISCO holding Board and Management in pursuing SISCO holding's strategic goals.
